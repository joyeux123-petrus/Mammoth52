🦣 About Mammoth52

Mammoth52 is a vibrant and visionary class of 52 dedicated students at Petit Séminaire Saint Léon Kabgayi, located in Muhanga District, in the Southern Province of Rwanda. United by faith, intellect, and purpose, Mammoth52 is more than just a class — it is a movement of future leaders committed to changing the world through spiritual works, social responsibility, and global impact.

Rooted in the values of excellence, humility, and brotherhood, Mammoth52 stands as a symbol of hope, transformation, and courage — walking together to build a better world, one spiritual step at a time.

🏆 Notable Achievements

🌍 International Recognition: TWAGIRAMUNGU Raymond Reponse (a.k.a. Wakanda Boy)

We proudly celebrate TWAGIRAMUNGU Raymond Reponse, known among his peers as Wakanda Boy, a brilliant member of Mammoth52, who has brought honor and pride to Petit Séminaire Saint Léon Kabgayi on the global stage.

  • 🧠 Pan-African Mathematics Olympiad (PAMO) participant, where he represented Rwanda with distinction, showcasing outstanding mathematical talent and problem-solving skills.
  • 🌎 Nominated to United World Colleges (UWC-USA), one of the world’s most prestigious international institutions focused on peacebuilding, academic excellence, and global citizenship.

Raymond’s achievements reflect the spirit and mission of Mammoth52 — to inspire transformation through excellence, discipline, and global engagement.
